Takikomi Gohan (Japanese Mixed Rice)

Description

Make this quick and easy recipe in your rice cooker. Use whatever ingridients you have in your fridge and enjoy the delicious and balance meal with the push a single button.

Ingridients

  • Rice
  • Meat of choice
  • Vegetable of choice
  • Soy sauce
  • Mirin
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Stock or Water

Steps

  1. Dice meat and vegetable
  2. Wash rice
  3. Add rice in rice cooker pot
  4. Mix stock, soy sauce, mirin, sugar, and salt together
  5. Add liquid mixture in the rice cooker pot
  6. Add vegetable in the rice cooker pot
  7. Add meat in the rice cooker pot
  8. Cook in rice cooker for 70 minutes, or use "Mixed Rice" setting is applicable
  9. Enjoy!
